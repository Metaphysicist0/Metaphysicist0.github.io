Hi! I am Tianliang Yao, an undergraduate majoring in Automation at the Department of Control Science and Engineering, [College of Electronics and Information Engineering](https://see.tongji.edu.cn/) at [Tongji University](https://www.tongji.edu.cn/).

My research aims to discover how Artificial Intelligence (AI) aids the field of healthcare. Currently, I mainly focus on two applications: surgical robotics and medical image processing. I seek to streamline and improve clinical workflows through this technology. I am equally fascinated by Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) for my laziness and other frontier technologies like soft robotics and continuum robotics (for their interesting shape and functions). I aspire to explore all of these areas through research in the years ahead, gaining exciting and valuable insights and making meaningful contributions.

Reach me at yaotianliang2003@gmail.com if you want to discuss research ideas or work together with me. Welcome!!!
